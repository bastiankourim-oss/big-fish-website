About Big Fish International

People Centric.
Since 1999.

An independent Franco-European cabinet built on one conviction: durable procurement performance is built with the people who deliver it, not around them.

Our story

Twenty years of substance.

Big Fish International was founded on a simple observation: most procurement transformations fail not because of flawed strategy, but because of flawed implementation by underprepared people.

Over two decades we have built proprietary frameworks — CRI, SRI, TOSCA — that assess, source, develop and deploy procurement talent with rigour and human intelligence. We work with fewer clients, more deeply. We measure outcomes, not activity.

Today our network spans 52 000 procurement professionals, 47 federations, and academic partnerships on three continents. The substance is not the problem. It never was.

Founder

Claus Nicolas Kourim

After twenty years at the intersection of procurement strategy, talent development and international management, Claus Nicolas founded Big Fish International to give senior procurement leaders the independent, people-first advisory they had been missing.

He holds a background in industrial engineering and international management, and has advised CPOs across the CAC 40, MDAX, AEX and Fortune 500. He is a regular speaker at CIPS, ISM and European procurement federation events.

History

Twenty-five years of milestones.

1999
Big Fish International foundedin Paris with a focus on procurement talent assessment and development for industrial companies.
2004
CRI methodology launched— a proprietary Competency and Role Index for procurement professionals, now used across 40+ countries.
2009
European expansion— partnerships with BME (Germany), NEVI (Netherlands), and CIPS (UK). Nine-language delivery capability established.
2014
TOSCA framework— a transformation methodology for procurement organisations integrating culture, process and people change.
2019
Lab-B52k launched— a research and innovation platform connecting 52 000 procurement professionals with academic and industry partners.
2024
300+ enterprise references— a milestone reached across CAC 40, Fortune 500, MDAX and public sector clients.
